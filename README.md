A personal weekly archive documenting major and minor technological developments across artificial intelligence, engineering, science, computing, and the global tech industry.

Entries are recorded every Sunday and contain **only headlines** — no explanations, opinions, or commentary.

The goal is to maintain a **long-term, signal-focused historical log of technological progress**.

## Purpose

- Build long-term awareness of technological progress  
- Practice **signal-over-noise information filtering**  
- Maintain a **public, time-indexed archive of innovation**  
- Create a structured historical record of weekly tech developments  

---

## Archive Structure

The archive is organized chronologically:


```
Sunday Tech Innovation Archive/
│
├── 2026/
│ ├── 01-January/
│ │ ├── 2026-01-04.md
│ │ ├── 2026-01-11.md
│ │ └── 2026-01-18.md
│ │
│ ├── 02-February/
│ └── 03-March/
│ └── ...
```

Each weekly entry contains structured sections such as:

- Major Headlines  
- Artificial Intelligence  
- Chips, Hardware & Infrastructure  
- Funding, M&A & Corporate Moves  
- Regulation, Security & Geopolitics  
- Platforms, Software & Media  
- Markets, Economy & Industry Trends  

Only relevant sections appear in each entry.
